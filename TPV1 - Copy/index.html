Newest College It girl

This is the ultimate Style Guide for the newest It girl. Use these tips and you are guarenteed the best outfit in the room. Being an "IT girl" in college, someone who is well-dressed, nice, and confident, is about more than just fashion. It's also about your attitude, kindness, and overall presence. Here are some tips to help you embody the IT girl persona: Define Your Style: Develop a personal style that makes you feel confident. It could be classic, trendy, bohemian, or a mix of styles. Invest in key pieces that reflect your taste. Wear What Makes You Feel Confident: Dress in outfits that make you feel good about yourself. When you feel confident in what you're wearing, it will show in your demeanor. Accessorize: Pay attention to accessories. A statement piece, such as a stylish bag, sunglasses, or jewelry, can elevate a simple outfit. Grooming and Personal Care: Take care of your personal grooming. Good hygiene and a well-kept appearance contribute to an overall polished look. Be Approachable: Smile and be approachable. A positive attitude and friendliness can make you stand out. Engage in Conversations: Be open to conversations and engage with others. Show genuine interest in people and make an effort to connect. Confidence: Stand tall and maintain good posture. Confidence comes from within, so believe in yourself and your abilities. Be Inclusive: Foster a sense of inclusivity. Be kind and open-minded, making others feel welcome and valued. Positive Energy: Radiate positive energy. Be optimistic, and try to find the good in every situation. A positive attitude is contagious. Be Genuine: Be yourself. Authenticity is attractive, and people are drawn to those who are genuine. Stay Informed: Stay informed about current events, trends, and topics of interest. Being well-rounded and knowledgeable can make conversations more engaging. Network: Build a network of friends and acquaintances. Attend social events, join clubs, and participate in activities to expand your circle. Balance: Balance trendiness with practicality. While staying on-trend can be fun, ensure that your outfits are appropriate for the college environment. Maintain Professionalism: Remember that college is a professional environment. Dress appropriately for classes, events, and any professional opportunities that may arise. Lead by Example: Lead by example in terms of kindness, work ethic, and style. Be a positive influence on others. Being an IT girl is not just about fashion; it's about embodying confidence, kindness, and authenticity. Strive to be the best version of yourself, and let your positive energy and style shine.

A little Vocabulary Breakdown
Trendy Styles Definition
Coquette Outfits that are composed of lace,pearls,and bows. They are often times majority pastel pink as well.
Elegant Outfits that are very suiting for the individual's body type, more modest. The outfits tend to be comprised of solid colors, more of a deeper tone.
Rocker Chic Outfits that involve studs, or statement jewelry that is oftentimes silver. Many pieces worn in this style have leather, or lace.
Minimalist Outfits that are monochromatic,lack design, and texture. A popular item to wear that falls within this style are oversized blazers.
