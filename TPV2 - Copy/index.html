Becoming the fashion icon of your college isn't just about following the latest trends; it's about mastering the art of expressing your unique identity through style. Start by building a versatile wardrobe that includes both timeless basics and a few trendy pieces each season. Mix and match these items to create outfits that reflect your personality and stand out from the crowd. Pay attention to the fit and comfort of your clothes true style icons know that confidence shines brightest when you feel good in what you're wearing.Accessorizing is your secret weapon. A statement necklace, a vintage belt, or a pair of eye-catching earrings can transform a simple outfit into something memorable. Don't forget the power of a great pair of shoes and a distinctive bag to complete your look.Stay informed about fashion trends, but don't be a slave to them. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to take risks sometimes the most unexpected combinations can be the most stylish. Remember, being a fashion icon is about leading, not following. Lastly, wear your outfits with confidence. Your attitude can make or break your look, so walk with your head high, smile, and own your style. Being a fashion icon is as much about how you carry yourself as it is about the clothes you wear.

A little Vocabulary Breakdown
Trendy Styles Definition
Coquette Outfits that are composed of lace,pearls,and bows. They are often times majority pastel pink as well.
Elegant Outfits that are very suiting for the individual's body type, more modest. The outfits tend to be comprised of solid colors, more of a deeper tone.
Rocker Chic Outfits that involve studs, or statement jewelry that is oftentimes silver. Many pieces worn in this style have leather, or lace.
Minimalist Outfits that are monochromatic,lack design, and texture. A popular item to wear that falls within this style are oversized blazers.
